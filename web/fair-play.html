15. Fair Play

Key Ideas:

When working with others as part of a group, or participating in a society to which we belong, we are guided by a sense of fair play, marked by:

  • proportional equivalence between benefits and contributions;
  • consistent rules that are applied uniformly;
  • graduated sanctions that distinguish between initial and accidental violation of norms, as opposed to informed, intentional and persistent violations.
