5. Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs

Link: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maslow's_hierarchy_of_needs

Image Credit: By Androidmarsexpress - Own work, CC BY-SA 4.0

Image Credit Link: https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=93026655

Maslow identified the following categories of needs, and indicated that lower-level needs (appearing first below) would typically take precedence, and that higher-level needs (appearing later) could only command energy and attention when lower-level needs were satisfied.

  • Physiological needs
  • Safety needs
  • Love and Social Belonging needs
  • Esteem needs
  • Cognitive needs, such as creativity, curiousity and meaning
  • Aesthetic needs
  • Self-actualization
  • Transcendence needs
