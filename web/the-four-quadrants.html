7. The Four Quadrants

Key Ideas:

We tend to perceive reality from four different perspectives, often referred as the four quadrants. Understanding these perspectives as four valid and complementary ways of perceiving the world is a good first step to obtaining an integral outlook. We can think of these as the individual subjective, the individual objective, the social objective and the social subjective.
